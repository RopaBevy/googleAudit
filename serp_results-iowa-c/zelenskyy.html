          Volodymyr Oleksandrovych Zelenskyy, also transliterated as Zelensky, is a Ukrainian politician who serves as the sixth and current president of Ukraine. Zelenskyy is a former actor and comedian. Zelenskyy grew up as a native Russian speaker in Kryvyi Rih, a major city of Dnipropetrovsk Oblast in central Ukraine. Wikipedia
          Born: January 25, 1978 (age 44 years), Kryvyi Rih, Ukraine
          Height: 5′ 7″
          Spouse: Olena Zelenska (m. 2003)
          Children: Oleksandra Zelenska, Kyrylo Zelenskyy
          Education: Kryvyi Rih Economic Institute (1995–2000), Kryvyi Rih Gymnasium #95 (1985–1995)
          Parents: Rimma Zelenskaya, Oleksandr Zelensky
